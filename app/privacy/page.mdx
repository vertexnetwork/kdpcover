# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-09_

## What we collect

**Nothing personal.** All cover calculations run entirely in your browser. Inputs (format, paper, page count, trim size) are stored only in the URL hash on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

## Anonymous analytics

We use Vercel Analytics, Vercel Speed Insights, and Microsoft Clarity to understand aggregate usage and improve performance. These services collect anonymized session data — page views, performance metrics, and interaction heatmaps — and do not identify individual users.

## Advertising

We do not run advertising. The site is monetised by sales of digital templates through Lemon Squeezy and by partner-tool referrals on the Recommended page.

## Data processors

- **Vercel** — hosting and first-party analytics
- **Microsoft Clarity** — heatmap and session-replay analytics
- **Lemon Squeezy** — digital-product checkout and delivery (only when you initiate a purchase)

## Cookies

We use no first-party cookies. Microsoft Clarity sets analytics cookies; Lemon Squeezy sets checkout cookies only after you click a buy button. No cookies are set for advertising.

## Your rights

Because we don&rsquo;t collect personal data, there&rsquo;s nothing to delete on our end. To opt out of third-party tracking, use the consent banner shown by the active ad provider, or set your browser&rsquo;s &ldquo;Do Not Track&rdquo; flag.
